Intelligent Integrity Analysis

AIPolygraph is more than a test. It’s a conversation with intelligence that can read between the lines. The system analyzes:

  • answers to strategic questions,
  • logical patterns and consistency,
  • signs of avoidance or deception,
  • emotional and lexical patterns of the response.

Questions are generated dynamically, based on previous responses. The full logic is a commercial and methodological secret.
