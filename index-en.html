What is AIPolygraph?

An online platform that analyzes user responses using artificial intelligence to detect signs of evasion, deception, involvement in corrupt schemes, or unwillingness to answer direct questions.

This solution is designed for the public sector, HR departments, security services, and anyone seeking a reliable method for integrity assessment.
