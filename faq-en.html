What is AIPolygraph?

AIPolygraph is an innovative integrity assessment system that uses artificial intelligence to analyze user responses to targeted questions.

Does AIPolygraph replace the traditional polygraph?

No, AIPolygraph is an alternative approach. It doesn’t require physical sensors but relies on linguistic, logical, and behavioral markers instead.

How accurate is the system?

Accuracy depends on the scenario, configuration, and type of user. In many cases, AIPolygraph demonstrates higher reliability than human subjective judgment.

Who can use this system?

The platform is designed for government agencies, HR departments, security services, and organizations seeking transparent personnel screening.

Can I test AIPolygraph?

Yes, there is a demo mode available. Visit the Demo page to try the system.

What kind of questions are asked?

Questions are dynamically generated based on the verification topic. Only examples are publicly available — the full database is confidential.

Will my data be saved?

In demo mode, no data is stored. In the full version, data storage is possible only with user consent or according to internal organizational policies.
